by the lord deputy and council @ a proclamation for the banishment of jesuit and priest etc. henry falkland it be well know by daily experience what intolerable mischief and inconvenience have grow upon this realm through the extraordinary resort of such person hither as be common call titulary popish archbishop bishop vicar general abbot prior dean jesuit friar seminary priest and other of that sect who seek to set up and maintain a foreign power and authority within this his majesty dominion the say bishop by pretence thereof have usurp and exercise ecclesiastical jurisdiction within this kingdom in all spiritual and ecclesiastical cause to the great derogation of his majesty imperial crown and the say inferior secular priest have likewise exercise all spiritual and sacerdotal function as christen marry and such like and by colour there of have take and exact sundry spiritual duty from such as be seduce by they to the intolerable grievance and impoverish of this poor nation and they all seek and daily endeavour to pervert the heart of his majesty subject and to draw they from the true religion here establish to blindness and superstition and to alienate their affection from their sovereign liege lord the king majesty and to subject they to the say foreign authority to the general disturbance of the common peace and tranquillity of this kingdom for reformation of which abuse and avoid of such seditious person sundry proclamation have be issue and publish in this kingdom some by immediate warrant from his majesty out of England and other by authority of the lord deputy and council of this kingdom for the time be thereby strict charge and command they forthwith or within some short time then after and now long sithence expire to depart out of this kingdom and never to return hither again unless they shall conform themselves to the religion here establish and repair to the church due and orderly upon pain of his majesty high indignation and such punishment as may just be inflict upon the wilful contemner of his majesty royal command all which notwithstanding his majesty be inform that the say popish titulary bishop vicar general priest jesuit and other person before mention have of late flock hither in great number then at any time heretofore and that such jesuit seminary priest and other priest friar vicar general abbot prior and bishop so ordain by foreign authority which do before time secret lurk in sundry part of this kingdom have of late time mo^e bold and presumptuous show and declare themselves in the use and exercise of their function in open assembly whereby his majesty subject have be general more seduce and confirm in their disobedience and obstinacy against his highness law and royal commandment in contempt of his majesty and the say former proclamation and be like to breed more and great mifchiefe if they be suffer to abide and continue here any long of which increase and insolence of the say popish bishop priest and other as aforesaid his majesty have take special notice have signify his high displeasure thereat and have thereupon command we to put all law in execution that be in force in this kingdom that inflict any punishment upon such as exercise any thing to extol or maintain the power or jurisdiction spiritual or ecclesiastical of any foreign prince or prelate within this kingdom and to revive the proclamation to remove and exclude all popish bishop prieft vicar general and other as aforesaid out of the same as the principal supporter and maintainer of the say usurp authority know you therefore that we in obedience unto his majesty royal command and for absolute warning to be give in this kind do denounce and in his majesty name strict charge and command that they shall all of they that be to say all titulary popish archbishop bishop vicar general abbot pryor dean jesuit friar seminary priest and other priest whatsoever regular or secular be make or ordain by any authority derive or pretend to be derive from the see of Rome or by any other foreign authority whatsoever depart out of this kingdom of Ireland forthwith or within forty day next ensue at the far after the date hereof and that no such titulary bishop vicar general abbot pryor dean jesuit seminary priest or other priest or friar ordain by foreign authority as aforesaid shall from and after the say forty day repair come or return into this kingdom upon pain of his majesty high displeasure and indignation and upon such further pain and penalty as may be just inflict upon they by the law and statute of this kingdom and upon the like pain we do in his majesty name express forbid all and all manner of person whatsoever within this kingdom to receive relieve or converse with be order by or receive instruction from any such popish titulary archbishop bishop vicar general abbot Pryor Deane Friar seminary priest or other priest which after the say forty day shall remain and abide in this kingdom or come into the same or any part thereof contrary to the intent of this proclamation and we do further in his majesty name declare publish and command that if any such popish titulary archbishop bishop vicar general abbot Pryor Deane Jesuit Friar seminary priest or other priest shall wilful abide and continue in this kingdom after the say forty day or shall voluntary repair or return into this kingdom or any part thereof or if any person or person shall receive relieve converse with be order by or receive instruction from any such popish titulary archbishop bishop vicar general abbot Pryor Deane Jesuit Friar seminary priest or other priest that then all and every provincial governor sheriff justice of peace mayor sovereign portrieff constable and all other his majesty officer and loyal subject in this kingdom shall use their best diligence and endeavour to apprehend all and every such popish titulary archbishop bishop vicar general abbot prior Deane Jesuit Friar seminary priest and other priest ordain by foreign authority as aforesaid and all and every of their receiver reliever and follower and they and every of they to commit to some safe and strict prison to the end such further order may be take for their punishment as to we the lord deputy and council shall be think fit and we require they and every of they to be severe to put this his majesty commandment in full execution as they and every of they will answer the contrary to his majesty who will call they and every of they to a strict account touch the performance of their duty in this behalf provide always that if any of the say titulary archbishop bishop vicar general abbot pryor dean jesuit friar seminary priest or other such priest whatsoever shall before the say forty day or within ten day next after he or their repair or return into this kingdom submit themselves before we the now Lo: deputy or to the Lo: deputy or other chief governor of this kingdom for the time be or other the governor of any province or before any of his majesty privy council in this kingdom and shall thereupon conform themselves and repair to the church due and orderly according to the intent of his majesty law that then it shall and may be lawful for all and every such popish titulary archbishop bishop vicar general abbot Pryor Deane Jesuit Friar seminary priest or other priest that shall so submit and conform themselves to abide and continue in this kingdom and to repair and return into the same and to have and enjoy the benefit of his majesty law and royal protection in as free and ample manner as any other loyal subject so long as they or any of they shall continue in such conformity give at his majesty castle of Dublin the one and twenty day of January 1623. Adam loftus Canc. Hen: Valentia Fra: Aungier Fra: blundel Geo shurley i. Blener hayset Dudley Norton Fra: annesley William parson Roger jones i. King Adam loftus God save the King
