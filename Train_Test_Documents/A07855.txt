mount taraghs triumph 5. july 1626. to the tune of the career king Charles be thou bless with peace and with rest who cause this assembly hither welcome you peer you earl of our shire you viscount and baron together Taragh the mount his joy do account be grate with your grateful presence noble than all at falkland his call to charles see you give your obeisance knight of great merit generous of spirit to Taragh you be welcome this day esquier and gentry the force of our country so march on in battle array spirit of great worth from the south to the north who heart do to honour aspire to stand with renown for Charles and his crown be your only delight and desire the squadron appear the troop they career from the foot of the hill to the top with force like a flood be main withstand their courage endure no stop king Charles to thy troop the hill of Taragh stoop that long a royal mansion have be with echo in the sky it shout and it cry long life to king Charles and his queen what can your foe expect but your blow and the force of your conquer hand if they be bend to take their event to approach to your shore or your sand back you will send they or altogether end they for the heaven and your right will uphold you captain why then come marshal your man as if Mars or king Charles do behold you falkland behold this army most bold most constant and loyal of heart to make up their fort or to maintain their port prepare to perform their part with heart they do show such homage as they owe and straight at a call do attend king Charles his pavilion against a arm million of foe they will stout defend the median-soyle unpatient of the foil be the heart of our native land mount Taragh of fame be the heart of the same whereon this royal army do stand that heart of her heart with every other part fair Ireland present to her king with trumpet and drum not fear for that (@) king Charles his triumph let we sing you gentleman and squire you knight of the shire your service with courage maintain in every degree let noble falkland see that your heart with your prince do remain then will be relate unto the king and state that Ireland breed subject most loyal you baron viscount earl give thanks to king Charles who cause this assembly most royal God save the King
