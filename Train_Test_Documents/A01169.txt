news late come on the last day of February 1591. from diverse part of France Savoy and Tripoli in Soria true translate out of the french and italian copy as they be send to right honourable person the king accompany with his own force and the martial biron have a long time lie about Chartres hope in the end to reduce the city to his devotion and perceive it a very hard thing to obtain the same by main force unless it be by surprise by reason of the strength of the same both by art and nature though there be very few soldier or none in it except the citizen and townsman thereof about the fourteen of February last he divide himself from the marshal Byron and feign to give over the siege and to go towards have first give out that he will go to besiege that town and go but two or three mile from Chartres the marshal Byron seem the next day to leave the siege and to follow the king as if he despair to do any good there which those of Chartres perceive they present with such soldier as they have resolve themselves to make a sally and to set upon the rearward of the marshal as he be march towards Dreur which according they do and issue out of the town most brave charge the marshal hinder troop the marshal at first seem to make head against they but soon after begin to retire himself and to give his enemy place and they nothing misdoubt of any such stratagem follow he amain continual skirmish until they come about two mile from the town in the mean time the king lie there about in ambush when he see his time enter between the town and the man thereof that follow the marshal and so courageous charge they behind and the marshal then turn face towards they again that in less than a hour that they fight they slay they all except thirty cuirass which very hardly escape the king follow the victory enter present one of the subburbe of Chartres the town man see this begin to make a motion of peace with he and offer to deliver he the town and fifty thousand crown upon condition that he will remove his force and place no garrison therein which the king refuse to do and thereupon prepare to batter the town in two place have resolve not to leave it until he have get it and it be very believe that he be in it already which if he be we be great bind to give God thanks for it for as much as those of Paris will great be hinder by the same for that they have most of their victual from those quarter thereabouts the lord Deguidieres deputy lieutenant for the king in the province of dalphinate have of late so hot pursue the duke of Savoy force that he enter his city of Ciamberie chief parliament town of Savoy and sack and spoil the same and then load with the rich prey thereof leave it forasmuch as he have not force sufficient with he to keep and the same and so withdraw himself home again without any loss the say lord Deguidieres return home from Ciamberie into dalphinate meet with the duke of Nemours and his force who know the absence of the governor think to have surprise some town and so charge he that he slay most of his cavalry and make he retire towards lion whereof the duke be governor whither the lord Deguidieres do so stout follow he that those of lion fear lest he will entrench himself in their suburb call Guillottiere adjoin to the gate of Rodan and be a very fair long and rich suburb set fire to the same and have clean burn it up but he withdraw himself into his own government where he have so honourable behave himself that he have in a manner reduce the whole province to the obedience and devotion of the king and be such a terror to his enemy that none dare stir the king of Spain have command certain cornet of horseman of Naples to come down from thence to the duke of Savoy that with they he may infest and trouble those of dalphinate in as much as they be horse of more swiftness and speed than he have any but the lord Deguidieres have meet with they have altogether defeat they that very few or none have escape he those of Geneva have late take a fort call the fort of saint Catherine not very far from Geneva which the duke have not long since cause to be build only to veer they and they have raze the same and have go spoil waste and burn so far as Nantua which be two day journey from Geneva those of air in Provence with all the country there about be in a mutiny and tumult against the duke of Savoy to who they have voluntary give themselves forasmuch as he go about to build a cirtadel in air which they by no mean will suffer the city of Marseille which with the great part of the country of Provence through the fair promise and instigation of the king of Spain have accept the duke of Savoy to be their protector and in sign of that advance the standard of the holy league which be a crucifix have of late upon the tumult of air revolt from he and erect the arm of France and do publish that it hold and will hold for the king of France certain jacopin friar of the city of Terracon situate upon the river of together with some evil mind citizen thereof have practise to give the city to the duke of Savoy of who they have receive many fair and large promise and have agree both of the mean and time the governor of the city have have some intelligence thereof and discover their whole platform one night before their treason shall have be effect cause the Iacopins and all their complice to be take and lay in sundry prison and that very night send certain of his company out of the town there to lie in a ambush until such time as they shall hear three stroke of a bell which be the watchword between the jacopin and the duke of Savoy lieutenant all this be thus appoint the duke force come to the city who so soon as they be near the gate at which they shall have be let in the bell be strike three time where present the ambush that be lay without the town assault they and those of the city which stand prepare to receive they open the gate and saily out upon they where in less than half a hour the duke man be so surprise in the midst be every man slay and their captain so sore hurt that he die two day after so be the city reserve for the king for who it hold still the duke of Savoy be of late fall into a consumption and lie very sick at Nizza so that his physician do despair of his recovery the lord Chatillon have of late have the lord de la Chastres in chase and have so near pursue he that he have drive he to Chasteauneuf where he do straight besiege he and be in hope to have he if he may have ordinance to batter the town by letter from Aleppo and Tripoli in Soria the twenty nine of December and the second of january last it be write to diverse of Venice that in the confine of Palestina be new start up two religious man who under colour of a reformation in religion and certain plausible order which they will have observe name themselves prophe send by God to illuminate the maumetist and to bring they out of darkness to eternal light who have so many follower that it be wondrous to tell and that one of they be go with ten thousand man to help the persian in his war against the great Turk and the other keep still about Palestina and daily conquer great
