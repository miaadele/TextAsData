@ a proclamation set forth by sir james Carol knight mayor of the city of Dublin where great inconvenience daily happen unto this city by reason of sundry disloyal wicked and malicious person who rather prefer their own private unlawful desire and their private commodity and profit then by observe of good law and order to procure the public commodity of the inhabitant of the same and do by their undutiful behaviour endanger themselves in the penalty and punishment of sundry law establish wherefore the say mayor intend the reformation of the same have think good aswell by the special commandment and direction of the right honourable the lord deputy and council establish for this kingdom as also by the advice of the say mayor and his assosiat the magistrate of this city to forewarn and give knowledge to such as have be the offender that they from henceforth shall not incur the danger of the law ordain for offence which by God permision he intend to put in due execution first the say mayor by the special comaundment and direction of the right honourable the lord deputy and council do in his majesty name straight charge and command all manner of jesuit and seminary priest and other priest authorize by the sea of Rome that be within the city of Dublin and franchise thereof contrary to the effect of sundry proclamation publish in that behalf do forthwith remove and depart from the say city and franchise so as they nor any of they nor any other of that kind be hereafter find within any part of the say city or franchise at their peril and further the say mayor by the comaundment and direction aforesaid do in his majesty name straight charge and command all and every inhabitant househoulder and other person and person whatsoever dwell within this city and franchise thereof that they nor any of they do from henceforth harbour keep or receive into any of their house any jesuit or other the priest aforenamed nor shall suffer the say jesuit or other priest or any of they to use or excercise their function contrary to the law of this realm neither prrmit or suffer any meeting or assembly of people in any of their house or chamber to hear the say jesuit or other priest excercise their say function upon pain of imprisonment of every such offendor till they and every of they shall be deliver by due course of law further the say mayor do in his majesty name straight charge and command all and every such person person within this city and suburb that do presume to teach and instruct child or other in any kind of learning or Docterine be not allow and license by the lord Archbyshopp of this diocese that they and every of they do forthwith forbear to teach and instruct within this city and franchise any child or other until they shall be allow and license by the say lord Archbyshopp and withal that no inhabitant or other person whatsoever within this city and franchise do from henceforth permit or suffer any such teacher or instruetor to teach within any of their house and chamber within this city and franchise upon pain of imprisonment and such further punishment as may be inflict upon they by the law and statute of this realm also the say mayor in the king majesty name do straight charge and command that no person or person inhabitant of this city or otherwise shall use any for staul regrate or engross to the hindrance of the market of this city in buy any wheat malt or other grain flesh fish butter egg cheese or any other victual or accate before the same come to the ordinary and accustom place for sell thereof there open and public to be sell upon pain of 20 s. and imprisonment and that no inhabitant in Fishtreete do permit any veal mutton or other flesh to be bring into their house to be keep for any country butcher or other person to the prejudice and disfurnish of the market upon pain of 20 day imprisonment aswell to the owner of the house where such flesh shall be find as also to the owner of the flesh and also forfeiture of the say flesh and the say mayor do by the advice aforesaid straight charge and command that no person or person inhabitant of this city and suburb shall from the 20 day of November next suffer any single-woman to keep his or their tavern in any sort upon pain of forfeit and pay ten pound for every such offence the one half to this city and the other half to such as shall inform the same and also the say mayor see the great number of fir and faggot reek that be place both within the city and also in the suburb whereby great inconvenience may arise if the same be not speedy prevent the say mayor by the advise aforesaid do straight charge and command all such person as have now at this instant any such fir and faggot or any other fuel subject to fir within this city to remove the same by the 20 day of November next and that none shall make any manner of fir or Faggot-Reeks hereafter within the say city upon pain of 40 s. for every such offence and further that no fir or faggot-reeke as aforesaid shall continue or be make within ten fathom of any house or tenement in the suburb of the city after the say 20 day of November next upon the like pain of 40 s. and that no stabler or other person from henceforth shall take any more for livery of any horse at hay and stable room for every 24 hour but 6 d. sterling upon pain of of 13 s. 4 d. for every offence and further for the avoid of diverse other inconuensence that may ensue the say mayor do in like manner charge and command that all country-butcher come to the market of this city hereafter with flesh to be sell shall bring with they the hide skin and tallow of all such beef mutton and veal as they bring to be sell upon pain of forfeiture of all such beef mutton or veal so buy or sell and that all such person shall bring the same to the fishamble there to be sell and not in the street out of the say shambles as heretofore have be accustom and not to be sell there before eight of the clock upon pain of imprisonment as well to the buyer as to the seller and withal that no free butcher of this city nor their servant be from henceforth find in the country shambles sell in gross or by retail any flesh upon any market day upon pain of 40 s. sterling and withal that the country butcher from henceforth shall continue in the country-shamble every market day to sell and retail their flesh from eight of the clock in the forenoon until one in the afternoon and no long upon pain of forfeiture of their say flesh and likewise that no person or person from henceforth do keep any disorder or miscule in any part of this city and suburb especial upon the Saboth day at any other day by day or by night upon pain of such punishment as be by the law provide and withal that no person or person do from henceforth play at tennis Stooleball or cudgel or use any kind of bodily excercise open in any street lane or place within this city and suburb on the sabbath day upon pain of imprisonment and that no prentice or servant of any citizen be from henceforth find play within any tennis-court or bowling-alley within this city and suburb upon pain of three day imprisonment in the stock to every offendor for every offence also the say mayor by the advice aforesaid do straight charge and command that no alewife or seller of strong ale or beer shall sell any strong ale or strong beer other then after the rate of one lawful seal wine quart for a penny sterling upon pain of forfeiture of ten shilling sterling and of ordinary beer or ale two wine quart for a penny sterling upon the penalty aforesaid and further the say mayor do by the advice aforesaid do straight charge and command that no inhabitant of this city or suburb shall utter or sell any wine beer or ale on any Saboth day until 11 of the clock in the forenoon and that during the same time no cook or other person shall sell any flesh fish or other dress meat either upon stall or otherwise upon pain of imprisonment for the space of twenty day and forfeiture of the same so buy or sell to be bestow upon the poor prisoner of the cas^ell or Newgate and that withal no person or person either free or otherwise inhabit within the franchise and liberty of this city shall from the 20 day of November next utter or sell either ale or beer in gross or by retail without the licence of the mayor of the say city for the time be first have and obtain in writing upon pain of forfeiture of 40 s. by every offendor in this case and imprisonment moreover the say ma^or do by the advice aforesaid straight charge and command that none of the citizen man or woman wear any irish apparel or such apparel as be prohibit by the law of this realm upon pain of such penalty as be by the same law make and provide also he further command that no cook of this city or suburb do or shall sell any kind of flesh or fish raw or undress without licence as aforesaid upon pain of forfeiture of x. s. for every offence and withal do straight charge and command that every man do use and have true weight and measure according the standard upon pain of such penalty and punishment as by the law be in that case make and provide and the say mayor do by the advice aforesaid straight charge and command that all idle sturdy and vagabond beggar and other young loiterer aswell man as woman have no lawful trade to live by or master to maintain they shall within 20 day after the publish hereof avoid this city and suburb upon pain of imprisonment for forty day and after to be whip and banish out of this city and further that no person or person do or shall come into the market on any market day either to cheapen or buy any grain before the bell be roung in the common hall upon pain of imprisonment of the offender at the discretion of the suid mayor and forfeiture of two shilling sterling by the offendor for every offence and that all person which buy or sell any corn from henceforth within the liberty shall buy and sell by this measure and not otherwise viz. the wheat peck seal to be three bushel of Winchester measure strict and the malt measure to be the bristol-band-barrel strict s. by every offendor in this nature and for the better effect hereof the say mayor have cause 30 peck for wheat to be make and distribute into the several ward of this city for avoid inconvenience and incertainty of the heap market peck heretofore use and further that no person or person of what quality or condition soever shall sell any flesh fish or any other victual or accate within this city or suburb except herb and fruit which must not be sell till after sermon-time in the afternoon upon the Saboth day upon pain of forfeiture of all such flesh fish victual and accate and that no carman carter brewer or their servant shall from the say 20 day of November come or go with any Ironbound-Cartes or carres within the wall of this city upon pain of forfeiture of twelve penny sterling for every offence also the say mayor by the advice aforesaid charge and command that no person or person shall from henceforth keep any swine cow or cattle within this city upon forfeiture of 20 s. sterling for every time offend in this nature and that no swine be keep unringe by any person or person in the suburb of this city nor suffer to come within the wall of this city upon the like forfeiture and further that no person or person from henceforth shall use within this city or liberty thereof the trade of broakage in sell or buy of any remnant of broadcloath stuff or other commodity or merchandise upon pain of forfeiture of forty shilling sterling by every offendor for every time offend and imprisonment during master mayor pleasure and likewise where many ungodly wicked and ill dispose person late come out of England and there be resident in this kingdom do use and continue that most curse and cruel trade of extreme interest and usury by grind the face of the poor in lend of money at rate far exceed judaical cruelty in that kind the say mayor b^ the advice aforesaid do straight in his majesty name charge and command all manner of person and person that heretofore have use or hereafter shall use that gripe trade of usury the say mayor purpose by the advice aforesaid to put all the say law and ordinance in due execution have cause this proclamtion to be print that it may be so public as none shall pretend ignorance of it and therefore command every alderman deputy alderman and constable in the say city and liberty to buy on of these for his full and perfect direction in the execution hereof date at Dublin the last day of October 1612. james Carol Maior Dublin GOD save the king
